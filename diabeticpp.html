Privacy Policy for DiabCare 360 App

Last Updated: 16-04-2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to DiabCare 360: Your ultimate diabetes companion. Continuous monitoring, personalized recommendations, and nearby hospital suggestions for effective diabetes management. Take control of your health today!

2. Health Information Collection

DiabCare 360 is designed to collect and store health-related information pertinent to diabetes management, such as glucose levels, medication usage, dietary habits, exercise routines, and other data relevant to your condition. This information is essential for providing personalized recommendations and insights to assist you in managing your diabetes effectively.

3. No Sharing of User Data

We do not share any user information with third parties.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will be available within the App.
